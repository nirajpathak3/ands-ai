Across 17 years I've worked through monolith-to-microservices migrations, real-time systems, and
multi-tenant SaaS, mostly in Node.js and TypeScript but comfortable picking up whatever the problem
needs. I'm equally happy going deep on a tricky production issue or stepping back to mentor and review.

I'd welcome the chance to talk about how I can help build and scale your integration platform.

## Usage notes

- Consistent framing: "backend architect (via Orangebits) at Ox Security." Drop one name if a specific
  application only wants the employer.
- Avoid AI tells: no "I am writing to express my keen interest," no "leverage," no generic enthusiasm.
- Always add one company-specific sentence in paragraph 1 (their product, domain, or a detail from the JD).
- Keep to one page. Shorter usually wins.
